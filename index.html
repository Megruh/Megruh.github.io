About

Let me introduce myself.

Hello! My name is Meg. I graduated from the Full-Stack Web Development course at Devmountain in May of 2022, and have found a new love for coding and web development. The tech industry has already proven to be the perfect atmosphere for me, as I am a perfectionist and thrive in environments that test my organizational skills and my technical abilities. I love learning and expanding my knowledge, and developing my newfound coding skills. Prior to becoming a web developer, I earned my bachelors and master's degrees in linguistics and forensic linguistics, respectively. I am passionate about language and understanding how it functions, and I think that passion translates well into learning and understanding new programming languages.

When I'm not building websites, I spend my free time camping, hiking, traveling, paddle boarding, hammocking, reading, painting, playing tennis and pickleball, working out, hunting for the best gelaterias in town, and hanging out with friends.
